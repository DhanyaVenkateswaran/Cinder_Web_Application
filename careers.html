Building the perfect career for one starts from choosing the right course for you. Most of the courses are stream-specific as they require the students to know the basics of the subject. More about Streams


Stream is essentially the subject group you choose in your higher secondary. As told before, Knowledge in certain subjects are deemed necessary by most courses so the stream you chose in Grade 12 plays an important role. The array of courses you can choose from varies depending upon your stream. It is important to note that certain courses are non-stream specific. Almost All Arts and Humanities courses fall under this category.


A list of Common Streams and their Core subjects are listed below :
  • Science Stream
    1. Biology group :
      Core subjects : Mathematics , Physics and Chemistry
    2. Computer Science group :
      Core subjects : Mathematics , Physics and Chemistry
    3. Pure Science group :
      Core subjects : Biology , Physics and Chemistry
  • Commerce Stream
    Core subjects : Mathematics , Economics , Accounting and Business Studies
  • Arts and Humanities Stream
    Most subjects are handpicked by the students of this stream so it depends on the subjects chosen by the students.
